Softening SOP

For 100 grams of hair

Steps

Phase 1: Preparation

  1. Thoroughly wash the hair with shampoo.
  2. Rinse well and completely dry the hair.

Phase 2: Boiling Stage

  1. Take 1 liter of water in a boiling vessel.
  2. Add 40 ml of WiseSoft40 to the water.
  3. Start boiling the solution.
  4. Put the prepared hair into the same vessel.
  5. Let it reach a boiling state on a medium flame.
  6. Once the water has boiled, remove the hair.
  7. Rinse and dry the hair.
  8. Once dry, press it with a hot iron.
  9. Wash the hair once more with shampoo, then rinse well to remove the shampoo.

Phase 3: Coating Stage

  1. Take another 1 liter of water.
  2. Add 3 ml of amodimethicone.
  3. Thoroughly brush the hair under the water so the amodimethicone is applied evenly.
  4. Once coating is complete, let the hair dry.

Phase 4: Final Wash & Dry

  1. Wash the hair a final time with shampoo in normal water.
  2. Put the hair out for final drying.
