Spreadsheet Tips:
  • Copy/Paste (Ctrl+C / Ctrl+V): You can paste multiple rows or columns copied directly from Excel straight into any cell.
  • Fill Down (Ctrl+D): Instantly copies the value from the cell directly above it.
  • Drag to Fill: Click an input, grab the blue square on its bottom-right corner, and drag to paint the value across other cells.
